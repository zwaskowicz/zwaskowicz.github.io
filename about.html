Why I took interest in programming and what I hope to achieve.

I view learning programming the same as learning how a car works. I might not ever work on one, but I should know how one works as it's fundamental to modern day life. Unlike car maintenance, however, I've been greatly enjoying my experience with programming! I love the logic puzzle, and seeing how my code forms the things I'm making. It's very satisfying for me to witness and be apart of. I plan on using programming primarily as a hobby, as it isn't my major, but also to understand the workings of the various devices I'll be handling in my ideal job. I am a biotechnology major, so I work with lots of intricate lab equipment who's inner operations I'd like to know more about. I come to programming with a passion for video games as well. I'm not very skilled with art, but I have a creative mind. Programming some fun projects may be a potential outlet for that, blending one of my current hobbies with a new one to help burn time and also offer me an outlet to express myself. I'm not just a big fan of videogames, but also tabletop RPGs. My imagination tend to trend in a fantasy direction as a result, with a love for creating mythical imagery from my thoughts and feelings.
