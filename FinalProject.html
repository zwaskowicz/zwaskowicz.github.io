ZW Clicker

waskowicz_clicker

This program is a short and sweet clicker game I made using my programming knowledge obtained over the course of my classes. Test to see how fast you can click! This game tracks the number of clicks (on release of the left mouse button for player 1 or spacebar for player2) you can make in the span of 20 in game seconds. You can play solo against your choice of three difficulties (arranged easiest to hardest from top to bottom), or play with a friend to see which of you can click the fastest! Player 1 clicks the left mouse button, and player 2 clicks spacebar. Keep the mouse within the game's boundary box for most consistent gameplay experience. Press the corresponding buttons to begin! Best played in khan. If not played there, refresh webpage to restart.

Idea inspired by the arm wrestling mini game from Monster Hunter World, where players online could simulate arm wrestling against eachother with the winner being who could press a controller button the fastest.
